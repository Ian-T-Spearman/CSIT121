Lab06

In this lab I needed to find a website to evaluate the design of, and how good the user interface and user experience is/was for people who actively use the website. Overall a lot of fun as I was able to choose a site that I use often that I find very useful. I did find however, that the accessibility rating was quite low. I would not have thought that op.gg would score like that, as I and many other people I know use it without a problem.
